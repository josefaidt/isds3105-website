About Us

Audio technology has been growing exponentially and it continues to bring joy to audiophiles throughout the world. The geographic local aspect of audiophiles was unheard of given the plethora of online communities dedicated to the subject, but as a group of friends were conversing in public about their favorite Sennheiser headphones, it attracted a fellow audio enthusiast. The group of friends decided to create an online instant message room to continue the conversation as technology evolved. Since then the online group collectively decided to meet once a month to discuss and present our favorite personal audio equipment. Officially created in May 2016, the Audiophiles of Baton Rouge is a club for audio, headphones, and budding audio technology (pun intended) enthusiasts.
