Welcome to Audiophiles of Baton Rouge club!

We are a club with a similar interest in headphones, home audio, and phenomenal audio in general. Collectively we are known as the Audiophiles of Baton Rouge (ABR) and we meet the second Thursday of every month at Lousiana State University in the Business Education Complex room 1253.
