Welcome to the Audiophiles of Baton Rouge Club!

We are a club with a similar interest in headphones, home audio, and phenomenal audio in general. Collectively we are known as the Audiophiles of Baton Rouge (ABR) and we meet the second Thursday of every month at 8:00pm. Lousiana State University has generously dedicated a meeting room for us in the Business Education Complex room 1253. Our club focuses on some of the best headphones for sale including wired, wireless, bluetooth, and gaming headsets. We do not discriminate amongst gaming platforms and often find ourselves chatting about the latest and greatest Xbox and PS4 headsets. Contact us today to learn more!
