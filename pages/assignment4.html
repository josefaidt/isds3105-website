To the right you will see a few pictures from Glacier National Park in northern Montana. After completing this web development course, I am leaving on a two week camping trip in Glacier with my brothers before one leaves for the military.

This national park is by far my favorite especially when it comes to the Summer weather. It can get up to 80° during the day and dip below 40° at night. Sometimes you can feel a 10° difference just by standing in the shade! My family went camping at Glacier NP in 2010 and we can't wait to return to our own Fortress of Solitude.
